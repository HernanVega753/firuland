
# FIRULAND

Small-scale game, with simple top-down RPG-like mechanics.
A puppy lives a great adventure (from his point of view), where he will make friendships, drive out great adversaries to protect his neighborhood and become everyone's protector!




## Tecnologías

 - GODOT ENGINE


## Objetivos

- Create a fun and stress-free game.
- Practicar el desarrollo de aplicaciones/juegos medianos, con control de versiones y desarrollar buenas prácticas.
